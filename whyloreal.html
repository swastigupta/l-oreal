WHY L'OREAL ?

For me, l’oreal is a company that has redefined beauty for all. It’s not what you are born with nor is it just your external appearance. L’oreal products give you room to experiment and match your inner self with the outer one.

Beauty for a better life defines beauty as something that has the power to inform, support and change perceptions; it has the power to generate confidence. Being able to connect with the values of the organization I work for is of utmost importance to me and I can establish that connection with L’oreal’s values of Passion, innovation, entrepreneurial spirit, open-mindedness and most importantly the quest for excellence.The Beautiful beginnings  

EXPECTATIONS FROM THE INTERNSHIP

Getting a live project that benefits L’oreal and provide me on the job training as well as familiarize me with the overall culture of the organization. I expect to accomplish challenging but realistic tasks by the end of 2 months. Learning from my peers and my mentor, I hope to develop professional competencies required in this field for success in future. A company with a vast global presence, I expect to get a glimpse of international exposure in terms of networking as well as understanding L’oreal’s strategies locally as well as globally.
